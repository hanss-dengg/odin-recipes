Teriyaki Chicken Recipe

Tasty and easy chicken recipe!

Takes only 20 minutes of time!

Ingredients:

  • Boneless chicken thighs
  • Light soy sauce
  • Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Honey
  • Sugar
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Sesame seeds (optional – for garnish)
  • Sesame seeds (optional – for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Mix the soy sauce, apple cider vinegar, honey and sugar in a bowl to make the teriyaki sauce. Set aside for later.
  2. Season chicken thighs on both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. In a medium sized frying pan, place chicken thighs skin side down and let it cook for 4 minutes on each side or until the chicken is browns.
  4. Remove the chicken and turn the heat to low. Add the teriyaki sauce into the pan and stir. Bring to a simmer for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Once it’s simmering, add the chicken back to the pan and coat it with the sauce. Let it cook for a few more minutes on medium low or until the sauce thickens. Serve the chicken over/with rice and your choice of veggies.
  6. Drizzle remaining sauce on top of the chicken and garnish with sesame seeds and green onion. Enjoy!
